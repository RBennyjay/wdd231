LagosOnlineProperties Site Plan

A comprehensive plan for the "Lagos Online Property Agent Platform" website.

Site Name

LagosOnlineProperties

This name was chosen for its direct relevance to the project's focus: providing online property services specifically within Lagos. "LagosOnlineProperties" is memorable, clearly indicates the geographic scope, and immediately communicates the website's purpose to potential users.

Site Purpose

The "Lagos Online Property Agent Platform" aims to be the premier online destination for individuals and businesses seeking to buy, sell, or rent properties within Lagos, Nigeria. The platform will connect property seekers with reputable real estate agents, offering a comprehensive database of listings including residential homes, commercial spaces, and land. Users will be able to search and filter properties based on criteria such as location, price range, number of bedrooms/bathrooms, and property type. Each listing will offer essential information like property address, pricing, key features, a descriptive overview, and contact details for the respective agent or owner. While primarily a search and information portal, it will also include a contact form for general inquiries or for users to express interest in listing their own properties. The site will provide a user-friendly interface to streamline the property search process and facilitate efficient communication between all parties involved.

Website Pages:

  • Home Page (index.html): This landing page will introduce the platform, highlighting its purpose and key benefits. It will feature a visually appealing hero section, showcase "featured properties" to entice users, and briefly explain how the platform works. Its goal is to provide an inviting entry point and guide users to the property listings.
  • Properties Page (properties.html): This is the core of the platform, displaying a dynamic grid of all available property listings. Users will interact with a robust filtering system to narrow down their search results. Clicking on a property will trigger a modal dialog, revealing more in-depth details about that specific listing.
  • Contact Page (contact.html): This page will provide various ways for users to get in touch, including a detailed contact form, email address, phone number, and business hours. It will also include links to social media profiles. Upon submission, the form will direct users to a separate success page displaying the submitted data.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: First-time homebuyer looking for an apartment in Lekki.

    Question: "As a first-time homebuyer with a budget of NGN 50,000,000, how can I find a 2-bedroom apartment for sale in Lekki Phase 1, and what are the steps involved in buying a property through an agent on this platform?"

  2. Scenario 2: Property owner wanting to rent out their commercial space in Victoria Island.

    Question: "I own a commercial office space in Victoria Island and want to list it for rent. How do I find a reliable agent on this platform to help me market my property, and what information do I need to provide for the listing?"

Color Schema

The chosen colors are designed to evoke professionalism, trustworthiness, and a clean aesthetic.

  • Primary Color: #007BFF (A shade of Blue)

    Usage: Used for headings (H1, H2), active navigation links, buttons (e.g., "Search," "Contact Agent"), borders for prominent sections, and potentially the site logo. This color signifies reliability and modernity.

  • Secondary Color: #F8F9FA (Light Grey)

    Usage: Primarily used for the general background of the website. This light neutral color ensures readability and provides a clean canvas for content.

  • Text Color: #343A40 (Dark Grey)

    Usage: Applied to all main body paragraphs, labels, and general text. This dark grey provides excellent contrast against the light background for optimal readability.

  • Accent Color: #28A745 (Green)

    Usage: Used for smaller, impactful elements such as "For Sale" or "For Rent" tags on listings, success messages, and icons that draw attention to specific features. This color represents growth and positive outcomes.

Typography

A combination of a strong, modern sans-serif for headings and a highly readable sans-serif for body text will be used to ensure clarity and a professional appearance.

  • Montserrat, sans-serif

    Usage: Applied to all primary headings (H1, H2, H3) and potentially important call-to-action text. Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif that offers a clean, contemporary feel, making headings stand out.
