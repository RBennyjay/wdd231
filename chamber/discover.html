Discover Lagos: The Centre of Excellence

Welcome to the Lagos Chamber of Commerce! As the vibrant heart of Nigeria's economy, Lagos is a city of unparalleled energy and opportunity. We are dedicated to supporting local businesses and fostering a dynamic community. Explore our rich history, diverse demographics, and exciting events to learn more about what makes Lagos truly unique.

A Glimpse into Lagos's Rich History

Once a fishing village, Lagos has evolved into one of Africa's largest and most dynamic megacities. Its history is shaped by ancient Yoruba kingdoms, colonial influences, and a resilient spirit of commerce and innovation. From the bustling markets of Lagos Island to the colonial architecture of Broad Street, every corner tells a story of transformation and growth.

Lagos by the Numbers & Its People

  • Population: Over 20 million residents (one of Africa's largest cities)
  • Average Age: Approximately 21-25 years (a very young population)
  • Vast cultural diversity, with vibrant arts, music (Afrobeats), and film (Nollywood) scenes.
  • Africa's leading economic hub, boasting a robust small business and entrepreneurial ecosystem.
